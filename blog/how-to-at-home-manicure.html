How to Do Your Nails at Home Like a Pro

Getting your nails done at a salon is a great experience, but sometimes, you just want to save money, relax at home, and do your own nails. Whether you're a beginner or looking to improve your DIY nail skills, this guide will walk you through how to do your nails at home like a pro!

Step-by-Step Guide to a Perfect DIY Manicure

Step 1: Remove Old Nail Polish

If you have any old polish, use a non-acetone nail polish remover to clean your nails. This helps keep your nails healthy and prevents dryness.

Step 2: Trim & Shape Your Nails

  • Use a nail clipper to cut your nails to your desired length.
  • File them into your preferred shape ‚Äì square, round, oval, or almond.
  • Buff the nail surface to smooth out any ridges.

Step 3: Prep Your Cuticles

  • Soak your fingers in warm water for 2-3 minutes to soften the cuticles.
  • Use a cuticle pusher to gently push them back (never cut them, as it can cause infections).
  • Apply cuticle oil to keep them healthy and hydrated.

Step 4: Apply Base Coat

  • Apply a thin layer of base coat to protect your natural nails.
  • Let it dry completely before moving to the next step.

Step 5: Apply Nail Polish

  • Apply two thin coats of your chosen nail polish.
  • Allow each coat to dry before applying the next to prevent smudging.
  • If you‚Äôre doing nail art, use a thin brush or stickers to create designs.

Step 6: Apply Top Coat

  • Once your nail polish is dry, apply a top coat to seal the color and add shine.
  • This also helps prevent chipping and makes your manicure last longer.

Step 7: Clean Up the Edges

  • Dip a Q-tip or a fine brush into nail polish remover and clean up any excess polish around the cuticles.

Step 8: Let Your Nails Dry Completely

  • Avoid touching anything for at least 10‚Äì15 minutes to allow your nails to dry completely.
  • You can also use quick-dry drops or dip your fingers in ice water to speed up the drying process.

üí° Moisturize your hands & cuticles daily ‚Äì This keeps your nails and skin healthy.
